About The College

History

Take A Look at How We Begin

Government Polytechnic Nandurbar was established in the historic city nandurbar in the year 2009. The institute is about 3 km from Nandurbar Railway station and Bus stand. Rickshaw facilities are also available from railway station and bus stand to Institute.

We are AICTE approved diploma programme focused on excellence.

Now, the institute conducts four AICTE approved three year regular diploma programme with total intake 240.

The institute facilities and capabilities are developed through its three component viz. capacity expansion, quality improvement and efficiency improvement.

Why Choose Government Polytechnic, Nandurbar.

The goal of the Institute is "To develop Personalities (technical manpower) with knowledge based, skill oriented and value added, with total and integrated perspective in the rural area"
